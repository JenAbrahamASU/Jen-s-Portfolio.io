A natural collector of ideas and information, she has always been drawn to exploring the stories which derive value from the world around her. Turning her passion into a profession, she is now in her second year of studying Communication Design.

For her, design is more than aesthetics—it’s about forming connection, avid storytelling, and creative problem-solving. She thrives on transforming her research and ideas into compelling visual narratives and innovative solutions.

A learner!


For her, this path is not just a career—it’s a way to bring ideas to life and connect with the world through the power of design.

Her journey began at Gokuldham High School and blossomed at Sophia College for Women, where she explored creativity and honed her voice.

Now at ISDI, she is steadily deepening her belief in the power of communication to bridge ideas and people.

The city of dreams!


For her, Mumbai isn’t just home—it is a collaborator, shaping her into a designer with a purpose.
